Inspirations

This is a slideshow of just a couple of the movies I hold close to my heart that I like to draw inspiration from in my own work. The Shining has been a movie that Ive studied for a long time, Stanley Kubrick as a director has such a distinct style. It transcends The Shining with Clockwork Orange another one of his works and creates a unforgetable film. Evil Dead 2 to me feels like one of Sam Raimi's most stylistically unique films, its a movie where becasue of how dissconected it is from the franchise due to legal bouts it allows it to come into its own.

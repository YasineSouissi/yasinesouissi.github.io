Did You Spill That? | 2023

Dive into the comedic chaos of "Did You Spill That," a short film packed with hilarious misunderstandings and outrageous accusations. When a mysterious spill on a laptop ignites a heated debate between roommates, tensions rise as blame is tossed around like a hot potato. With sharp wit and snappy dialogue, the film navigates through absurdity and absurdity, leaving viewers in stitches. From competitive rowing to rogue felines, every twist and turn keeps you guessing until the very end. So, buckle up for a wild ride of spilled secrets and spilled liquids in this uproarious comedy.

Wizard THERAPY | 2024

I was the Sound Designer and one of the 2 editors on this project. To give a Description "Wizard Therapy" takes viewers on a whimsical journey through the fantastical world of magic and mayhem. When a group of friends embarks on a quest, tensions rise as they confront their own inner demons and grapple with the consequences of their actions. From lost companions to chaotic encounters with mystical beings, the group navigates through hilarious misadventures and unexpected twists. With humor and heart, "Wizard Therapy" explores themes of friendship, redemption, and the absurdity of life's challenges. Prepare for laughter, spells gone awry, and a tale that proves sometimes the greatest magic of all is found within ourselves.

Kappa Alpha Kill | 2024

In this short film I was the post production supervisor as well as one of the foley artists. In the story "Kappa Alpha Kill" we plunge viewers into the dark and twisted world of sorority rituals and deadly consequences. As a group of friends indulges in a bizarre tradition of drinking pig's blood to pass their final exams, tensions rise and secrets unravel. When the stakes escalate to a virgin sacrifice, the line between reality and superstition blurs, leading to a chilling climax of betrayal and bloodshed. With shocking twists and gripping suspense, "Kappa Alpha Kill" delivers a gripping tale of academic pressure, moral dilemmas, and the deadly price of ambition.

Can I Hit That? | 2024

This is a project I wrote along with a friend as well as directing and editing it. "Can I Hit That?" takes viewers on a wild ride through the absurd and unpredictable world of vape flavors. When a group of friends gathers to indulge in some recreational vaping, things take a bizarre turn when they encounter an eight-year-old vape with a questionable flavor. As tensions rise and suspicions mount, the friends find themselves embroiled in a hilarious and increasingly chaotic quest to uncover the truth behind the mysterious vape flavor. Filled with witty banter, outrageous antics, and unexpected twists, "Can I Hit That?" delivers a rollicking comedy that will leave audiences in stitches from start to finish.

Smoke and Mirrors | 2023

In this short film I was one of the writers and came up with the idea for the story. "Smoke and Mirrors" offers a behind-the-scenes glimpse into the chaotic world of indie filmmaking. As a group of passionate filmmakers struggles to capture the perfect shot for their latest project, tensions run high and tempers flare. From failed attempts at levitation to malfunctioning equipment, everything seems to go awry as the deadline looms. With witty banter and comedic mishaps, "Smoke and Mirrors" explores the ups and downs of creative collaboration and the pursuit of perfection in the face of adversity.

A True Crime | 2022

On this project I co-directed and co-edited with a friend. "A True Crime" delves into the murky depths of a real murder case, exploring it through the lens of a true crime documentary. As the filmmaker and his crew embark on this chilling journey, they uncover shocking details and unexpected twists. But as tensions rise and the investigation takes a dangerous turn, the crew must confront the grim reality of their pursuit. With its blend of suspense and introspection, "A True Crime" offers a captivating and thought-provoking exploration of justice, fame, and the human psyche.

Prepare For a night of films from one of my favorite directors, Sam Raimi. In "Evil Dead 2," Raimi takes us back to the infamous cabin in the woods for a terrifying and darkly comedic tale of demonic possession. Brace yourselves for ghastly sights, outrageous gore, and the iconic chainsaw-wielding hero, Ash Williams, portrayed brilliantly by the incomparable Bruce Campbell. But the night doesn't end there! In "Army of Darkness," Raimi unleashes a medieval fantasy epic like no other, as Ash finds himself transported back in time to battle an army of the dead. With its blend of horror, action, and slapstick comedy, this cult classic showcases Raimi's unique vision and boundless creativity. So join me as we celebrate the genius of Sam Raimi, a filmmaker whose bold imagination and devilish charm have left an indelible mark on the world of horror cinema. So join me on October 20th for this double feature that will leave you laughing, screaming, and cheering for more!
